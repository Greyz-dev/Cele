McMillan Visuals

Paul McMillan is the owner and creator of McMillan Visuals. Paul’s passion is storytelling. Through the perfect combination of light, equipment, and creativity, he is able to capture the unique stories of every person he works with. With his extensive experience, creativity, and skill Paul is able to capture and tell the stories of couples committing to spend their lives together, of business owners who put their noses to the grindstone every day to make the world a better place, and of anyone who has a story to be told.

“I grew up as a quiet young boy, I would speak when I had something I found important to say, otherwise I would just listen and observe.” - Paul McMillan
